# Reports

This folder holds feature-scoped operational reports.

## What Goes Here

- Audit outputs (context audits, plan audits, harness audits)
- Cross-cutting verification or investigation reports
- Session closeout packets that span multiple plans

## What Does Not Go Here

- Reports belonging to one plan (colocate inside that plan's task folder in `active/` or `completed/`)
- Feature-specific reports (use `process/features/{feature}/reports/`)
- Durable project knowledge (belongs in `process/context/`)

## Naming Convention

`{topic}_REPORT_{dd-mm-yy}.md`
